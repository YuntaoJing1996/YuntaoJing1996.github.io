About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the China University of Geosciences, specializing in Earth Science under the supervision of Professor Zengqian Hou. My research focuses on the enrichment and fractionation behaviors of REEs as they are transported from continental to marine systems.

Research Interests

  • Marine REEs: Micro-scale distribution of REEs on minerals, marine REEs source, REE enrichment mechanisms.
  • Asian interior weathering: REE behaviors; Microbiological effects (sulfate-reducing bacteria); Isotope geochemistry, Climate change impacts, REE cycling from continental to marine systems.
